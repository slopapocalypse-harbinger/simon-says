Simon Says: Mindbreaker Odyssey

A deep tactical idle-adventure designed to melt your brain (in a good way).

Mission Briefing

Primary Objective: Grow your operation by leveling up, unlocking every zone, forging artifacts, and surviving rising threat.

Quick start (first 5 minutes)
  1. Use Scout to build early Insight + Credits with low risk.
  2. Roll 1–2 quests, then play toward their targets for bonus rewards.
  3. Buy Autonomous Drone and Fusion Reactor early to improve passive income and energy recovery.
  4. When your Energy is healthy, use Breach Node or Deep Expedition for bigger payouts and XP.
  5. Spend shards on Synthesize Artifact for permanent boosts that scale your whole run.
What each section does
  • Operations: Your active buttons. Each action costs energy and pushes your run forward.
  • Zones: Harder zones unlock by level. They increase rewards, but danger and losses also scale up.
  • Active Challenge: Timed events that appear randomly. Completing them gives big bonus rewards.
  • Mission Feed: Live log of successes, failures, and system messages.
  • Quest Board: Optional objectives. Completing quests grants credits, insight, and shards.
  • Upgrades: Persistent run boosts bought with credits.
  • Artifacts: Permanent power spikes crafted from shards.
  • Achievements: Milestones with bonus resources; useful long-term goals.
Resource tips
  • Energy is your action fuel. Keep some in reserve for challenges.
  • Threat rises as you act. If it overflows, you take a major energy hit.
  • Insight + Credits are your core economy. Balance spending on upgrades with shard generation.
  • Shards are rare and best saved for artifact crafting once costs rise.
